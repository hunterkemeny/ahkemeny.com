My Favorites

Here are some of my favorite resources, papers, and tools that have significantly influenced my work:

Favorite Papers

  • "Attention Is All You Need" - Vaswani et al. (2017) - Revolutionary work that introduced the Transformer architecture
  • "The Unreasonable Effectiveness of Mathematics in the Natural Sciences" - Eugene Wigner (1960) - A philosophical foundation for my work at the intersection of AI and physics
  • "Computing Machinery and Intelligence" - Alan Turing (1950) - The foundational paper that sparked my interest in AI

Favorite Tools & Resources

  • PyTorch - My go-to framework for neural network research and experimentation
  • Weights & Biases - Essential for experiment tracking and visualization
  • arXiv - The lifeblood of staying current with AI research
  • Jupyter Notebooks - Perfect for prototyping and sharing research ideas

Favorite Books

  • "The Elegant Universe" by Brian Greene - Beautiful introduction to modern physics
  • "Gödel, Escher, Bach" by Douglas Hofstadter - Mind-bending exploration of consciousness and computation
  • "Deep Learning" by Ian Goodfellow, Yoshua Bengio, and Aaron Courville - The definitive textbook

Inspirational Quotes

"In physics, you don't have to go around making trouble for yourself. Nature does it for you." - Frank Wilczek
